Salmon Bites

Food decription

Traditionally cubed cut pieces of salmon cooked and served either as they are or over a bed of rice

How you'll get it done

Ingredients

  • 1 pound salmon, skin removed, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
  • 1/4 cup honey mustard

Steps youll follow

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Combine salmon, oil, paprika, salt, and pepper in a small mixing bowl. Toss to combine. Place seasoned salmon in the basket of the air fryer.
  3. Air fry for 4 minutes.
  4. Using a basting brush, lightly brush honey mustard over salmon pieces. Stick a toothpick in each bite.
  5. Air fry until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 4 minutes more.
  6. Transfer to a serving plate and serve immediately.
